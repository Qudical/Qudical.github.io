About the game

COMING HOME is a retro slasher horror game, with a focus on home defense.

In the dead of night, you hear someone lurking outside - but you are not defenseless. Watch the cameras, barricade your doors, and have your gun at the ready - anything to keep the Sundown Rippers from claiming their next victim.

Coming to Windows, Linux & Steam Deck
